1. Introduction

routeart ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

routeart is designed to be privacy-first. We do not store your personal data on our servers.

  • Strava Data: We access your Strava activities only when you explicitly connect your account. This data is processed locally on your device to generate visualizations.
  • Authentication Tokens: Your Strava access tokens are stored securely on your device (using secure storage mechanisms provided by the OS).

3. How We Use Data

We use the data solely to:

  • Display your activities within the app.
  • Generate the route visualizations you create.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the Strava API to fetch your activity data. By using routeart, you are also subject to Strava's Privacy Policy.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@routeart.app.
